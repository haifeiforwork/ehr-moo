1. Meeting room Outline

This screen is to reserve meeting room and notify meeting attendee of meeting schedule via SMS and MAIL. User can search meeting list in which he/she is designated as attendee, and manager can add, modify, and delete meeting room.

Meeting room screen

① Click date on the calendar on the left menu to link to daily reservation search screen for the relevant date.

2. Today’s reservation status

2.1 Today’s reservation status view (daily search)

This screen is to check today’s reservation time by meeting room. Click empty time to reserve the relevant meeting room on the relevant time.

Create Schedule

① Can move to the previous date for search
② Can move to the next date for search
③ Conversion between weekly view and monthly view
④ Click empty time to directly move to reservation screen
⑤ Place mouse point over the reserved time to display reservation detail layer. Click it to pop up reservation detail view screen.

2.2 Weekly search

This screen is to search weekly meeting room reservation. Like daily search screen, click empty time to move to reservation screen. Click the reserved time to move to daily search screen

Weekly search screen

① Can move to the previous week and next week
② Conversion between daily search monthly search screen
③ Click the reserved time to change screen to daily search screen
④ Click empty time to reserve meeting room on the relevant date.

2.3 Monthly search

This screen is to search monthly meeting room reservation.

monthly search

① Can move to the next month and the previous month
② Conversion between daily search and weekly search
③ Click the reserved list to display reservation detail view screen in pop-up.
④ Click date to change screen to reservation screen for the date.

2.4 Meeting room reservation

This screen is to reserve meeting room. General user can make a reservation for the period from today to the coming two weeks and only manager can make a reservation for date after two weeks. In addition, if reservation time is duplicated, alert message of ‘Reservation Not Available’ will be displayed to guide you

meeting room reservation

① Meeting room [division] and Meeting room [group] After selecting the conference room belonging to the group to select the final.
② Select meeting time. Reservations must be an administrator, except two weeks.
③ Mail and SMS to inform the conference participants to set the time. Can be set to [before 30 minutes, 1 hour, 2 hours, 1 day]
④ Book a meeting room in addition to the logged in user can change another person. (Agency can be registered)
⑤ Add meeting attendee..
⑥ Add attach file.

2.5 Meeting room reservation list

In this screen is to search meeting room reservation list by period. You can search total list or meeting room reservation made by yourself or for which you are attendee. Manager can modify or delete all reservation. In case of person who made a reservation, he or she can search, modify, or delete his/her reservation details

meeting room reservation list

① After setting the period available for retrieval (default is one week today, it belongs)
② The whole or a related, Book, and participants can search for options
③ Administrators will see the bulk delete button.
④ Administrators will see a delete button in the list.
⑤ Click the book title and the book can be converted to View More Details View, you can modify, or delete a Book.

2.6 Meeting room list

This screen is to search meeting room information such as facilities or capacity of meeting room, contact point of meeting room manager.

meeting room list

① Manager can delete meeting room. (Checkbox is visible only to manager.)
② Manager can add meeting room. (Button is visible only to manager.)
③ Click meeting room name to move to the relevant meeting room detail view screen.

2.7 Meeting room detail view

This screen is to view meeting room detail. Manager can change this screen to modification screen by clicking ‘Modify’ button.

meeting room detail view

Only meeting room marked with ‘Y’ can be currently available e for reservation.
Manager can modify various meeting room information in the modification screen and, if necessary, he/she can modify ‘Available /Not available’ to change a meeting room to ‘reservation not available’ status

2.8 Meeting room registration and modification

Manager can add meeting room in this screen. He/she can also add image file that shows meeting room facilities, meeting room manager and location of meeting room. (meeting room change screen is same with meeting room register screen.)

meeting room registration and modification

① You can select or add division and group and then you can add meeting room.
② You can select ‘Not available’ to set a meeting room to ‘reservation not available’ status.
③ Set meeting room manager.
④ You can upload image file that shows location of meeting room.
