1. Overview

This is to share idea of someone and receive supplementation opinion with some informal spirit. Best Idea that is applied for commercialization or real work will be provided to share until commercialization or application information after suggestion stage.

Idea Management progress stage

When an idea is proposed in Idea Management Screen, the suggested Idea will become concrete suggestion through sharing and participating of many users. Suggested Idea with recommendation of the most of users will be selected as the best Idea through users’ participation. Added to that, if it is applied to a user’s work, it becomes an applied Idea. Best idea or Idea applied to work through recommendation of many users will be reviewed by person in charge of the work and/or manager. In case person in charge of the work and/or manager decide that commercialization is possible, the idea will be selected for commercialization and then the idea will be migrated to the related business division. In case of idea for which commercialization is not possible, it will be stored in Idea DB to be used as reference at the time of new Idea proposal.

Configuration of Main Idea Management screen

The figure below is the configuration of main Idea Management screen. Besides left menu area, diagram will be provided on the right side to grasp Idea activity status at a glance. The followings will be provided for recently suggested Idea.

① Idea activity status : suggested Idea will be divided into ‘in progress, recommended idea, best Idea, Idea applied to work, Idea selected for commercialization, and completed Idea’ to provide activity status.
② idea in progress : Display recently registered 5 Ideas for easy check of recently suggested Ideas. One of 'best', 'Adopt', 'reject', 'selected for commercialization', or 'commercialization is completed' will be displayed in front of each Idea for easier check of idea with other users.
③ Move of list of idea in progress : Click ‘Move’ button to move idea to menu in which they can check all Ideas.

3. Idea proposal

There is a built-in web-editor for easier and more convenient idea composition and idea can be proposed in free format.

① title : Enter idea title. Title is required item.
② contents input : Enter idea contents. Since there is a built-in web-editor, users can edit Idea contents easily and conveniently.
③ file attachment : In case there is file related to idea, user can select and attach file.
④ tag input :
Enter keyword related to an idea. If possible, please correctly enter related keyword for Idea application and sharing. Keyword can be entered up to max 10 keywords, and use comma (,) between keywords as a separator. Entered keyword can be changed or modified any time.

4. Idea search and application

This function is to share opinion about suggested Idea with many users, recommend it, and to apply it to work in order to make a suggested Idea to a concrete idea.

① recommendation : Click ‘Recommend’ button to recommend the relevant Idea.

5. Selection and completion of commercialization

Person in charge of work and manager will review best or idea applied to many users’ work. In case person in charge of work and manager decides that an idea is good for commercialization, he/she will select the idea for commercialization. In addition, it can be completed after progress of commercialization. In case of idea for which commercialization is impossible, it will be stored in Idea DB as reference for new Idea proposal.

Selection for commercialization by manager

In case a manager decide that commercialization of an idea is possible after reviewing it, he/she will click 'select for commercialization' button on the right bottom of search screen and register review opinion to designated it as an Idea selected for commercialization. In case commercialization is impossible, click 'Idea reject' button to save it in Idea DB for future Idea proposal. Idea selected for commercialization can’t be modified or deleted by the registrant. In case of rejected Idea, registrant can delete it but he/she can’t modify it.

① Selection for commercialization : The manager may click ‘Select for commercialization’ button to select the idea for commercialization.
② review opinion input : Manager can enter review opinion during commercialization selection or rejection to send reason of selection or rejection to idea registrant and searcher.

Search of Idea selected for commercialization

Idea selected for commercialization will have a mark of selected for commercialization in front of title. It is to show that the idea is selected for commercialization out of many ideas and to share it with other users. Manager can cancel idea selected for commercialization by clicking ‘Cancel’ button. After completing commercialization, the manager can click ‘Commercialization is completed’ button to change the Idea status to commercialization is completed status. Idea that completed commercialization will be archived for sharing of commercialization use information. Idea that completed commercialization can’t be modified or deleted by the registrant.

① select for commercialization display : Idea selected for commercialization will have a mark of selected for commercialization in front of title.
② manager review opinion : This is to check review opinion of commercialization selection. Manager can modify review opinion later.

6. My Idea activity

This function is to provide information on My Idea activity status. You can check out the details from suggestion activity, contribution activity ranking to the relevant activity. This function provides list of idea proposed by me for easy check of current progress status of My Idea. It also provides list of idea applied to my work for easy check of progress status of idea applied by me.

① My Idea activity status : This is to divide My Idea activity status into suggestion, contribution activity to provide the relevant detail.
② Total Idea proposal ranking : This is to provide information on top 3 rankings according to total Idea contribution activity score.
③ Idea suggested/applied by me : This is to provide 10 recently registered ideas among ideas suggested and applied by me. Click ‘View 10 more’ button to display 10 more ideas on the bottom.

① best Idea provider Top 10 : Provide information on top 10 users with high idea suggestion activity score in accordance with idea proposal activity policy
② best activity user Top 10 : Provide information on top 10 users with high contribution activity score in accordance with idea contribution activity policy.

7.2 Idea commercialization status

'Idea commercialization status' provides function to grasp current progress status of suggested Idea at a glance. The search function by each period will allow users to check idea activity status and list of suggested idea of each period.

② Idea list : Display list of idea registered in search period.
