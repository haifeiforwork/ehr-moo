1. Survey outline

Survey is to induce participation of many respondent through easy preparation of web survey and distribution and to provide survey responding status and respond analysis.

In addition, it provides various function options for survey preparation in order to get correct respond to meet survey goal.

Survey support outline

    • 1-1) Make Survey
      • 1-1-1) Register Default Infomation
      • 1-1-2) Register Survey Question
    • 1-2) Request Approval Survey
      • 1-2-1) Request Approval Survey
    • 1-4) Survey
      • 1-4-1) Start Survey
      • 1-4-2) Send Mail
    • 1-5) Check Process
  • 2. Approver
    • 2-1) Survey Approval Process - Reject, Approve
  • 3. Respondent
    • 3-1) Get Mail
    • 3-2) Answering
Major features
  • It supports easy and convenient survey preparation functions such as survey preview, survey re-preparation.
  • It increases answer ratio and induces more meaningful survey result through provision of various setting options such as anonymity survey, survey refuse, survey opening.
  • It provides 20 types of various questions to induce survey respond that meets the goal of each use of question.
  • It supports smooth survey progress through early close of survey, real time provision of answer ratio, etc.

3. Major functions of survey

3.1 Survey preparation – basic settings

Survey provides function to set survey period and target in the stage of preparation of basic information and to set various options required for survey progress

Preparation of basic settings of Survey

① Enter survey name.
② Select preface input type. text type is set as default. Check image RADIO BOX to upload image
③ Select survey progress schedule.
④ Select survey anonymity. In case ‘Yes’ is selected, anonymity of respondent will be notified to users, and respondent information won’t be save.
⑤ Select opening of survey result. In case it is selected as ‘closed’, survey result won’t be provided to users.
⑥ Select display of survey refuse button. In case ‘Display’ is selected, respondent can enter survey refuse reason on the bottom of SURVEY, and if you click survey refuse button you can refuse responding the survey.
⑦ Select survey target. In case corporate is selected, corporate survey will be conducted. In case individual setting is selected, screen for target setting will be searched.
⑧ Save the entered basic survey information.

Setting of Survey target

① Select user. Search user name entered in textbox.
② Address book for user selection will be searched.
③ Delete selected user.
④ Select group. Search group name entered in textbox.
⑤ Address book for group selection will be searched.
⑥ Delete selected group.

3.2 Survey preparation – question preparation

SURVEY provides about 20 of various question type, example is provided for each question to help user to write question with easy. In addition, add, delete, and move of questions is provide in intuitive UI to enhance user’s convenience

Preparation Stage of survey question

① Enter survey group name.
② Enter survey group description.
③ Delete survey group.
④ Add survey item on the top of the selected survey item.
⑤ Delete current survey item.
⑥ Add survey item on the bottom of the selected survey item.
⑦ Add survey group.
⑧ Add survey question group.
⑨ Select survey question type.
⑩ Select number of answers for survey question.
⑪ Select answer type.
⑫ Select required answer.
⑬ Select question display.
⑭ Preview of currently prepared survey.

Registration of survey questions

① Select survey question type.
② Show example of selected survey question.

3.3 Survey approval

SURVEY provides function of survey approval function. A user can request for conducting survey prepared question and ready to survey manager through survey approval function and post the survey after getting approval for efficient progress of survey

screen to request for survey approval

① Request for survey approval to manager.

survey approval menu

① Search is available only to survey manager, and it is a menu to search survey requested for approval.
② Search list of survey requested for approval.

survey approval screen

① Approve survey to be posted.
② Reject survey. In case of rejection, requestor for survey approval will modify survey and then request for approval again.

screen to complete survey approval

① Post survey. Sending option of survey mail can be set when a user post survey.
(Mail sending option and time: immediately, after 1, 6, and 12 hours).

3.4 Survey participation

Survey provides menu for survey in progress, current user can search total survey, and Survey provides real time answer ratio of survey in progress

Survey participation menu

① Search list of survey in progress in which user is included as survey target.
② Search answer ratio of survey in progress.
③ Search posting period of survey in progress.

Survey screen

① Submit prepared survey.
② Refuse survey submission. (Select survey refuse in basic survey information)
※ Survey writer will click ‘close’ button in survey in progress to close survey earlier than expected time.

3.5 Survey result

Survey provides survey result of survey in progress and closed in the same form, and survey in progress has function to re-send mail to non-respondent in order to increase survey answer ratio. In addition, answer ratio of each date and analysis of answer ratio of survey item are provided. In addition, you can download result as an excel file.

Survey result

① Send mail to non-respondent.
② Move to search screen to search survey result detail.

View of Survey result detail

① Download survey result as an excel file.
