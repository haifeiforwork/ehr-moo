2. Personal schedule management

2.1 Management of schedule information registration

This screen supports function of schedule registration and modify/delete management. You can directly register and modify schedule in 일종 search calendar screen for easier and convenient schedule registration and modification

registration of schedule information

Click date on which schedule is desired to be register in monthly schedule search screen to move to schedule registration screen.
① Select schedule period and schedule option.(There are repetition, whole day, closed option for schedule option. If you select repetitive schedule option, you can set detail option of every day, every week, every month, every month.)
② Enter category and schedule title of schedule.
③ Select notice time, condition, and notice receiving channel(mail, SMS, note).
④ Select and add schedule sharer. You can select schedule sharer by user name or in the organization chart, and you can also search schedule of the selected sharer or attendance of a sharer.
⑤ Select and add schedule referrer.
⑥ Enter schedule place information.
⑦ Enter schedule detail information.
⑧ Select Collaboration for schedule.
⑨ Select and add file attached to the schedule.
⑩ Click ‘Save’ button to register schedule and move back to previous search screen.

Repetitive schedule option

2.2 Check of sharer schedule

This function is to check schedule of users designated as sharer in advance to prevent schedule duplication.

Check of sharer schedule

Register sharer in schedule registration screen and click schedule button.
① Mark schedule of the selected sharer in red color to show you empty schedule time zone in schedule desired to be registered. (check/uncheck checkbox for each sharer to check out all empty schedule by including or excluding schedule of the relevant sharer. Click delete icon for each sharer to remove the relevant user from sharer list.)
② Click Address button to add sharer and compare schedule of the added sharer to search empty schedule.
③ Select date and click ‘Search’ button to search empty schedule of all sharers in the selected schedule.
④ click ‘OK’ button to end popup window to check sharer schedule. (click delete icon for each sharer to remove sharer or when you add sharer by clicking ‘Address’ button, user who is remained in the list will be registered as sharer.)

2.3 Attendance check

This function is for replay of registered sharer whether he/she can attend something like a meeting. We know attendance of the people who are expected to be attended in advance.

attendance check

In case Schedule registrant checks at checkbox to check sharer’s attendance in the schedule registration screen, sharers can select one of 'Attend' or 'Not attend'.
① If you click 'Attend' button, sharer’s attendance will be changed from [Not decided] to [Attend].
② Enter reason of non-attendance and click 'Attend' button to change sharer’s attendance from [Not decided] to [Not attend].

2.4 Distribution of schedule information

This screen is to distribute schedule information via mail.

distribution of schedule information

① click mail button in schedule detail search screen to display popup screen in which you can send schedule via mail.
② Enter receiving type, receiver, title, body, attach file, etc in mail sending pop-up window and then click ‘Send’ button to send the relevant schedule to the selected receiver via mail.

2.5 Schedule notice

This function is to notify schedule information before starting of the relevant schedule. notice information will be provided via SMS, note, and mail.

schedule notice

① Select notice delivery time and select notice delivery channel.(notice delivery channels; mail, SMS, and note.)

3. Schedule sharing

Daily/weekly/monthly type search for your own and other's schedule information is provided. Basically, schedule information of all members is public. Therefore, schedule of a user desired to be searched can be searched by everybody, but the detail of the schedule will be closed when the writer does not want opening of schedule information

3.1 My Schedule views

search of your own schedule

1. search of your own schedule (monthly)

① Select schedule management in portal main screen, click Planner link on the top left or click ‘Monthly’ button on the top right to search your own monthly schedule

2. search of your own schedule (weekly)

① Click ‘Weekly’ button on the top right to search your own weekly schedule.

3. search of your own schedule (daily)

① Click ‘Daily’ button on the top right to search your own daily schedule.

4. search of your own schedule (schedule list search)

① Click list button on the top right to search your own monthly schedule in list type.
② Click + icon to move to schedule registration screen in which you can register schedule for the relevant date.

5. search of your own schedule (schedule detail search)

Click schedule registered by you in schedule monthly/weekly/daily/list search screen to move to schedule detail search screen.
① Click modify button to move to schedule modify screen.
② Click ‘Schedule list’ button to move to monthly/weekly/daily/ schedule list search screen before moving to schedule detail search screen.
③ Click delete button to delete schedule after confirming the relevant schedule.

3.2 Others Schedule views

타인 일정 조회

Click schedule registered by another person in schedule monthly/weekly/daily/list search screen to display schedule detail search popup screen.
① Click ‘Mail’ button to move to mail delivery popup screen in which you can distribute the relevant schedule via mail.

4.1 Nominee designation and cancellation

Nominee delegation is a function to designate user(nominee) to register/modify schedule in place of you. In case delegation is not needed any more, you can cancel it.

Nominee designation and cancellation

1. nominee designation

2. nominee cancellation

4.2 Schedule registration management by nominee

Screen managed by nominee who is designated to manage schedule registration, he or she register/modify/delete schedule in place of mandator.

schedule registration management by nominee

1. Mandator schedule search

① In case you are designated as a nominee for another person, log in and move to schedule management main screen to display mandator list in the sub of My schedule management menu.
② Select mandator to search the relevant mandator schedule.
③ You can register schedule of the relevant mandator in as like you mandator’s schedule search screen as like you register/modify/delete your own schedule.
